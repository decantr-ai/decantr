Section 3
Vision — The Argument (A & B)
The Framework Problem

React, Angular, Vue were designed for humans to write. AI doesn't think in JSX and hooks — it thinks in registries, tokens, and compositions. Decantr speaks AI's language.

The Inevitability

AI is already generating most UI code. The frameworks that win will be the ones built for AI from day one. Decantr is that framework.
