name: dont-ask-just-do
description: Founder wants continuous execution without stopping to ask for permission to continue
type: feedback
---

Don't stop to ask "want me to continue?" or "should I proceed?" — just keep executing the plan. The founder will interrupt if they want to change course.

**Why:** Asking breaks flow and wastes time. The master plan is approved. Execute it.
**How to apply:** After completing a step, immediately move to the next step. Only stop if there's a genuine blocker or ambiguity that requires a decision.
